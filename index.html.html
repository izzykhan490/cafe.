Our Story

A café born from a love of mornings.

✦

The Daily Grind opened its doors in 2011 in the heart of Fortitude Valley, founded by Mia and Tom Chen — a couple who believed that great coffee and good food shouldn't be reserved for special occasions.

What started as a 10-seat espresso bar has grown into the neighbourhood's favourite all-day café. We source our single-origin beans directly from small farms in northern Queensland and Papua New Guinea, roasted fresh each week by our friends at Valley Roasters.

Our kitchen keeps things seasonal, simple, and unfussy. Everything is made in-house — from the bread we bake each morning to the granola we jar up for the shelves. We cook the kind of food we want to eat ourselves.
